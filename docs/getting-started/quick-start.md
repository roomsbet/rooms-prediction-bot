# Quick Start

Get started with ROOMS in under 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Start the Bot

1. Open Telegram
2. Search for `@roomsbet` (or your bot username)
3. Click **Start** or send `/start`

## Step 2: Create Your Wallet

Your wallet is automatically created when you first start the bot. **Important:** Screenshot your private key when shown - you won't see it again!

Your wallet is secured by Turnkey's enterprise-grade key management system.

## Step 3: Deposit SOL

1. Click **Wallet** in the dashboard
2. Click **Deposit**
3. Send SOL to the displayed address
4. Wait for 1 confirmation (usually instant with Helius RPC)

## Step 4: Place Your First Bet

1. Click **Enter Available Rooms**
2. Browse active prediction markets
3. Select a room
4. Choose **YES** or **NO**
5. Enter your bet amount
6. Confirm your bet

## Step 5: Win SOL

- Winners are paid automatically after settlement
- You'll receive a push notification with your results
- Check your balance in the Wallet section

## Creating Your Own Room

Want to create a custom market?

1. Click **Create Room** in the rooms list
2. Choose market type (Coin Price, Pump.fun Market Cap)
3. Set your question/title
4. Set target value
5. Select settlement time
6. Deploy and your room goes live instantly!

## Commands

- `/start` - Open dashboard
- `/wallet` - View wallet and transactions
- `/bets` - Track your active and historical bets
- `/referrals` - View referral code and earnings
- `/won` - See your winning history
- `/rooms` - Browse available rooms
- `/create` - Create a new prediction room
- `/rules` - Learn how ROOMS works
