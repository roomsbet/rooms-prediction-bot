# User Room Creation

## Create Your Own Markets

Anyone can create custom prediction markets on ROOMS in seconds.

## How to Create a Room

1. Click **Create Room** in the rooms list
2. Choose market type:
   - **Coin Price** - Bet on price movements
   - **Pump.fun Market Cap** - Bet on token market cap
3. Set your question/title
4. Set target value (price or market cap)
5. Select settlement time (15min, 30min, 1h, 2h, 4h, or custom)
6. Deploy and your room goes live instantly!

## Market Types

### Coin Price
- Track any cryptocurrency price
- Set target in USD
- Choose price or market cap tracking
- Examples: "Will BTC reach $100k?", "Will ETH hit $5k?"

### Pump.fun Market Cap
- Track Pump.fun token market cap
- Set target market cap
- Real-time updates every 3 seconds
- Examples: "Will $TOKEN hit $10M market cap?"

## Settlement Options

### Time-Based
- Settles at scheduled time
- Oracle checks final price
- Standard settlement method

### Instant Settlement
- Settles when target is hit
- No waiting for scheduled time
- Instant payouts

## Room Guidelines

### Good Room Titles
- Clear and specific: "Will SOL reach $165 by 6pm?"
- Include timeframe: "Will BTC hit $100k in the next hour?"
- Be accurate: Use correct token symbols

### Bad Room Titles
- Vague: "SOL moon?"
- Missing details: "Price target"
- Misleading: Wrong token or price

## Host Rewards

Room creators earn:
- **1% host fee** from winning payouts
- Rewards distributed automatically
- No action required

## Tips for Success

1. **Choose popular assets** - More bettors = bigger pools
2. **Set realistic targets** - Too easy/hard reduces interest
3. **Use clear titles** - Help users understand the market
4. **Monitor your rooms** - Engage with bettors
