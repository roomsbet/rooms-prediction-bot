# Referral System

## How It Works

Invite friends to ROOMS and earn rewards from their betting activity.

### Your Referral Link

Every user gets a unique referral link:
```
https://t.me/yourbot?start=YOURCODE
```

Share this link with friends. When they sign up and bet, you earn rewards!

## Reward Structure

- **5% of protocol fees** from referred users' bets
- **Lifetime earnings** - No expiration
- **Instant payouts** - Rewards credited automatically
- **No limits** - Refer as many as you want

## Example

Your friend signs up via your link and bets 10 SOL:

1. They win → Payout: 9.7 SOL (after 3% fees)
2. Protocol fee: 0.2 SOL (2% of 10 SOL)
3. Your reward: 5% × 0.2 SOL = **0.01 SOL**
4. Credited instantly to your balance!

## Tracking

View your referral stats:
- **Referred Users** - Total signups via your link
- **Total Rewards** - Lifetime earnings from referrals
- **Active Referrals** - Users who are actively betting

## Best Practices

1. **Share widely** - Post on social media, groups, forums
2. **Explain the value** - Help friends understand ROOMS
3. **Stay active** - Active referrers get more signups
4. **Track performance** - Monitor your referral stats

## Terms

- Rewards only from protocol fees (2% of bet amount)
- Only applies to bets placed by referred users
- Rewards credited automatically after settlement
- No minimum withdrawal for referral earnings
