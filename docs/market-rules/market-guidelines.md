# Market Guidelines

## Room Creation Rules

### Allowed
Cryptocurrency price predictions  
Market cap predictions  
Token-specific markets  
Time-based markets  
Custom oracle markets  

### Not Allowed
Markets on illegal activities  
Markets on personal information  
Markets designed to manipulate  
Spam or duplicate rooms  
Misleading or false information  

## Content Guidelines

### Titles Must Be
- Clear and specific
- Accurate (correct tokens/prices)
- Not misleading
- Appropriate for all audiences

### Examples of Good Titles
- "Will SOL reach $165 by 6pm today?"
- "Will BTC hit $100k in the next 24 hours?"
- "Will $TOKEN reach $5M market cap?"

### Examples of Bad Titles
- "SOL moon?" (too vague)
- "Price target" (missing details)
- "Will BTC hit $1?" (misleading)

## Settlement Rules

### Fair Settlement
- Based solely on oracle data
- No manual intervention
- Transparent and verifiable
- Recorded on-chain

### Disputes
- All settlement data is public
- Oracle sources are verifiable
- Contact support for issues
- Appeals reviewed fairly

## Betting Rules

### Limits
- Minimum bet: Usually 0.05 SOL
- Maximum bet: Varies by room
- Room capacity: Usually 5-10 players
- One bet per user per room

### Cancellations
- Bets cannot be cancelled
- No refunds after confirmation
- Double-check before confirming

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Room removal
- Account restrictions
- Loss of host fees
- Permanent ban (severe cases)

## Reporting

Report violations:
- Inappropriate content
- Manipulation attempts
- Technical issues
- Settlement disputes
