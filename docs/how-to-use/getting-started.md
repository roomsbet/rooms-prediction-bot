# Getting Started

## First Steps

1. **Start the Bot** - Send `/start` to @roomsbet
2. **Create Wallet** - Automatically created, save your private key!
3. **Deposit SOL** - Send SOL to your wallet address
4. **Browse Rooms** - See active prediction markets
5. **Place Bets** - Choose YES or NO and bet amount

## Understanding the Interface

### Dashboard
- **Wallet Balance** - Your SOL balance and USD value
- **Active Rooms** - Rooms you've bet in
- **24h Stats** - Your profit/loss and win rate
- **Quick Actions** - Access all features

### Room Details
- **Title** - The prediction question
- **Status** - OPEN, LOCKED, or SETTLED
- **Players** - Current bettors and capacity
- **Pools** - YES and NO pool sizes
- **Time Left** - Until settlement

## Your First Bet

1. Click **Enter Available Rooms**
2. Select a room that interests you
3. Read the question carefully
4. Choose **YES** or **NO**
5. Enter bet amount (minimum shown)
6. Review and confirm

## After Betting

- **Check Status** - View in "My Bets"
- **Wait for Settlement** - Automatic when conditions met
- **Receive Notification** - Push alert with results
- **Collect Winnings** - Automatically added to balance
